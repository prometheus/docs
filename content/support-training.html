Support & Training

Prometheus is an open source project. Training resources on this page are listed for your convenience only. These resources are independently created, and their content is not governed or verified by the Prometheus project's maintainers.

If you'd like to add your training resource, see Add a resource.

Courses

This list is provided in alphabetical order.

Learn how to Monitor with Prometheus

A set of courses. Master Prometheus, including the data model, PromQL, configuration, Grafana, alerting, instrumenting and practical deployment.

Delivered by Robust Perception Training.

Monitoring Systems and Services with Prometheus

This course leads new Prometheus users through many of its major features, best practices, and use cases. Course participants are expected to have basic experience with Linux/Unix system administration, as well as some development experience in Go and/or Python.

Delivered by Linux Foundation Training

Add a resource

Anyone who has developed a Prometheus training program or offers related services can add themselves to this page if they meet the following requirements.

For courses and training programs:
  • The course must have a publicly available syllabus or outline on the page linked
  • If the course is instructor led, you must provide credentials (a link to your LinkedIn profile or resume will suffice)
For consultants or businesses:
  • Consultants must provide credentials (a link to your LinkedIn profile or resume will suffice)
  • Businesses must outline what Prometheus-related services they provide on the page linked

To submit, open a pull request against this page in the prometheus/docs repository. Include all the information requested in the PR description.
